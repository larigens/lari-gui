About Me

Born and raised on the coast of Brazil. I moved to the US in 2020 and since then, all my choices have led me down a path towards tech. I like to think coding chose me.

I enjoy learning new tricks and tools that make my work and my life easier, and that is how my career as a software engineer began. Solving problems and making things aesthetically pleasing is what I call "my guilty pleasure"! I love challenging myself to become better at my craft, so if you have any advice or feedback on how I could improve, please let me know!

I am currently working on a personal project called Coffee Break, which is a website that will allow users to find information about everything I'm learning as I work to become a software engineer. The main goal is to pass on my knowledge to future programmers.

In my free time, I like to play video games and workout to de-stress after hours of de-bugging hah

Create. Code. Design. Debug. Play. Workout. Have Fun !important;
